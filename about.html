MY STORY

I'm Mskithedj—a world-class athlete turned world-building DJ, and this journey is more than a career move. It's a story of transformation, healing, and rediscovery.

For years, I lived and breathed competition as a nationally recognized athlete. When injury brought my track career to a sudden stop, I was forced to face a life without the sport that had always defined me.

And then, I turned to music. DJing gave me the same adrenaline rush, the same sense of rhythm and timing, but with a freedom and emotional depth I had never felt before. Music became my therapy, and the decks became my new arena.

Now, every time I step behind the decks, I bring that same discipline, focus, and heart that once made me a champion. Whether I'm spinning live on TikTok, rocking a pop-up, or throwing a party that feels like a movement, I'm doing it with full intention and unmatched energy.

This is just the beginning. The vision is bigger, the purpose is deeper, and the vibes are unstoppable. Come be part of this journey with me.

Let's build something unforgettable—together.
