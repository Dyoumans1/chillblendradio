Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you DJ?

I DJ a wide range of events including clubs, festivals, corporate events, private parties, weddings, and brand activations. My versatile style allows me to adapt to any environment and audience.

How far in advance should I book?

For club events, 1-2 months in advance is typically sufficient. For private events and weddings, I recommend booking 3-6 months ahead. For major events and festivals, 6+ months is ideal to ensure availability.

Do you travel for gigs?

Absolutely! I'm based in Los Angeles but available for worldwide bookings. Travel and accommodation costs will need to be covered in addition to my standard fee for locations outside of the LA area.

What equipment do you provide?

I bring my own DJ controller and laptop to all events. For larger events, I can coordinate with you or your venue for sound system requirements. I'm comfortable with various setups including club standard CDJs.
